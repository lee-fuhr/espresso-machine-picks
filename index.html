How we choose our picks

We do not run our own lab tests or timed trials. Instead, we base recommendations on a few reliable things: the brand‘s long-standing reputation in home espresso, the volume and sentiment of real owner feedback across major retailers and forums, and the consensus from trusted, published expert reviews. This approach helps us surface machines that have proven themselves over years of actual kitchen use. We also look at what commonly breaks or frustrates owners after the first few months. A machine might look great on paper, but if its pump fails or its steam wand clogs frequently, it won‘t make the cut. We prioritize models with readily available parts and straightforward maintenance, because a great espresso machine is one you can still use a year from now.
